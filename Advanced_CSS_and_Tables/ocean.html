About the Ocean

Physics is a major obstacle in the field of ocean exploration. At great depths, the ocean exhibits zero visibility, extremely cold temperatures, and crushing pressures, according to oceanographer Dr. Gene Carl Feldman of NASA's Goddard Space Flight Center.

Ocean Preservation

Organization creating and expanding technology to remove plastic from the oceans. We employ two different approaches to accomplish this goal: cleaning up plastic waste that has already accumulated in the ocean and won't go away on its own, and intercepting plastic in rivers to reduce the amount of pollution entering the water.
