The rose has over three hundred different species. The rose falls in two different categories of woody perennial flowering plant Rosa or Rosacena for the flower it bears. The Juiliet rose is the most expensive rose costing 2.3 million pounds. The smell comes from microscopic glands.

Roses are one of the oldest flowers. You can eat roses. Their fragrance is used in perfumes. Each rose color has a different meaning. The rose is the U.S. National Flower.
