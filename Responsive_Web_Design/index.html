Ocean Recycle

How do we stop littering? There are numerous effects of ocean pollution on humans and marine life, both directly and indirectly. Ocean excess debris uses oxygen to break down over many years, which reduces the amount of oxygen in the water. Ocean animals like sharks, whales, dolphins, and penguins perish due to low oxygen levels in the water. Oxygen depletion is also a result of excess nitrogen and phosphorus in seawater. An area of the ocean may turn into a dead zone where very little marine life can survive when there is significant oxygen depletion there.
